South India Temple's Tour

DAY 01: ARRIVE CHENNAI

Upon arrival at airport, meeting and greeting after we will transfer to pre-booked hotel. Afternoon Visit Ashtalakshmi Temple, Kapaleeswarar etc. Overnight stay at hotel. Temples in Chennai:- Shri. Ashtalakshmi Temple and Shri. Kapaleeswarar Temple.

DAY 02 : CHENNAI - TIRUPATI BY ROAD 162 KMS 4 HOURS DRIVE

After breakfast, drive towards Tirupati. Tirupati - A temple and pilgrim town. On arrival, check in at hotel. In the afternoon proceed to sigthseen. Overnight stay at hotel Temples in Tirupati:- Sri Kalahasti Temple, Sri Venkatwswara Temple ( tirupati balaji ) and Sri Padmavati Temple

Afternoon sightseeing of Mahabalipuram, the famous for the seven pagodas. Overnight at the hotel. Temples in Mahabalipuram:- Shore Temple

DAY 04: MAHABALIPURAM - TIRUVANNAMALAI BY ROAD (150 KMS 03 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast, drive towards Tiruvannamalai. On arrival, check in at the Hotel. After freshening up we will go for sightseeing. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Tiruvannamalai :- Arunachaleshwara Temple, Virupaksha Cave, Annamalaiyer Temple and Sri Ramana Ashram

DAY 05 : TIRUVANNAMALAI - CHIDAMBARAM BY ROAD (140 KMS 03 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast drive to Chidambaram. On arrival, check in at the Hotel. After freshen up we go sightseeing. Overnight at the Hotel. Temples in Chidambaram :- Thillai Nataraja Temple, Thillai Kali Amman Temple and Tirunallurpperumanam Temple

DAY 06 : CHIDAMBARAM - KUMBHAKONAM (73 KMS 02 HOURS 30 MIN DRIVE)

After breakfast, continue driving towards Kumbha Konam. On arrival in Kumbhakonam, check in at your Hotel.Overnight stay at Hotel. Temples in Kumbhakonam:- Navagraha Temple , Papanasam, Swami Malai Murugan Temple, Kumbeswarar Temple and Nageswarar Temple

DAY 07: KUMBHAKONAM - TRICHY BY ROAD (100 KMS 3 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast, drive towards Trichy, Check in at the hotel. After fresh up, we will go sightseeing and Overnight stay at the hotel. Visiting Temples in Trichy Sri Ranganatha Swami Temple, Brahma Pureeswarar Temple, Rock fort Temple and Jambukeswarar Temple

DAY 08 : TRICHY - MADURAI BY ROAD (135 KMS 03 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast, drive to Madurai city. Check in at the hotel, After fresh up Watch evening AARTI at Meenakshi Temple. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Madurai :- Meenakshi Amman Temple, Tirupparankuram Murugan Temple and Koodal Azhagar Temple

DAY 09: MADURAI - RAMESHWARAM BY ROAD (120 KMS 3 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast drive to Rameshwaram, a city of swell and gentle sand dunes, embellished with the delicacy of casuarina trees and stark palms. Check in at the hotel, after fresh up we will go sightseeing. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Rameshwaram:- Sri Ramanatha Swamy Temple, Agnitheertham, Panch Mukhi Hanuman Temple and Ramarpadam Temple

DAY 10: RAMESHWARAM - KANYAKUMARI BY ROAD (350 KMS 7 HOURS DRIVE)

After early breakfast, drive to Kanyakumari. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel. Evening visit at temples. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Kanyakumari:- Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Devi Kumari Temple

DAY 11: KANYAKUMARI - TRIVANDRUM BY ROAD (80 KMS 2 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast, Drive Trivandrum, check in at hotel. We will go for visiting tamples . Overnight stay at Trivandrum. Temples in Trivandrum :- Suchindram Temple, Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Pazhavangadi Ganapathy Temple

DAY 12 : TRIVANDRUM - COCHIN (195 KMS 5 HOURS DRIVE)

After breakfast drive to Cochin, On arrival check in at the hotel. After fresh up, we will go for visiting temples. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Cochin:- Oachira Parabrahma Temple, Sri Chottanikkara Bhagavathy Temple and Ernakulam Siva Temple

DAY 13 : COCHIN - GURUVAYUR - COCHIN (70 KMS ONE WAY)

After breakfast drive to Guruvayur to visit the famous temple. Evening back to Cochin. Overnight stay at the hotel. Temples in Guruvayur:- Guruvayoor Shri Krishna Temple, Vadakkum nath Shiva Temple at Thrissur and Vishnumaya Temple

DAY 14 : GURUVAYUR - COCHIN AIRPORT DROP (70 KMS 2 HRS DRIVE)

Today departure transfer to Cochin Airport to board flight for onward Journey.
