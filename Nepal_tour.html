North India with Nepal Tour Package

Delhi - Jaipur - Agra - Jhansi - Khajuraho - Varanasi - Kathmandu - Delhi Overview With prominent destinations, North India coupled with Nepal becomes an inevitable tourist preposition. Nestled in the lap of Himalayas, Nepal is worth touring especially for those on a lookout for unbridled fun and adventure. It is indeed one of those rare combinations that render an unforgettable staying and holidaying experience. Destination Covered: Visit Red Fort, Jama Masjid and monuments in Delhi Excursion to Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace - Jaipur Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur Embark with the beautiful Taj Mahal Witness the temples of Khajuraho Enjoy the boat ride at river ganges - Varanasi Visit Durbar Square, Patan and Bhaktapur – Nepal Visit Tibetan Refugee Craft Centre

Day 1: Arrival at Delhi

This day begins with the arrival in Delhi One of the major tourist destinations, the glorious capital of India is heart-warming. You will be assisted to the hotel by our representatives to relax. After taking your lunch, you can start an exhilarating tour of OldDelhi and New Delhi in the afternoon. We cover all the major destinations in Delhi. Jama Masjid ,the stupendous Red Fort and Mahatma Gandhi’s cremation memorial are some of the sites to get enthralled

Day 2: Delhi – Jaipur

We drive from Delhi to Jaipur. In six hours we reach there by road. After that check inthe hotel and spend the rest of the day to relax. You will be staying overnight in the hotel.

Day 3: Jaipur Sightseeing

This day is specially kept to enjoy the stunning Amber Fort. As the outer appearanceis rugged, quite different from the alleviating ambience, it is one of the fittest places toenjoy. Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, City Palace Museum and other places are visited on this day. Night is planned to be spent in Jaipur itself.

Day 4: Jaipur – Agra

We take the road, which takes us nearly 5 hours to reach Agra. On the way, you willstop at the chivalrous Fatehpur Sikri. This antique city has sustained most of itsstructures. After reaching Agra, you will spend the rest of the evening in the hotelOvernight stay in Agra itself.

Day 5: Agra Sightseeing

This day is special. In the early morning hours, we will witness the sunrise and hat tooin amidst the breath-taking ambience of the Taj Mahal. Built by Emperor Shahjahan inthe memory of his wife Mumtaz, this is one of the most impressive places to enjoy.Also visit Agra Fort, Itimad-Ud-Daulah, Tomb of Akbar, Chini ka Rauza etc

Day 6: Agra – Jhansi –Khajuraho

In the early morning hours, we take you to Jhansi. After the arrival, we take you to Khajuraho, which takes nearly 3 hours by road. You also earn an opportunityto witness Orcha and experience the beauty of the excellent temples there

Day 7: Khajuraho – Varanasi

In the early hours of morning, we head towards the legendary Khajuraho Temples. In the afternoon, we catch a flight to Varanasi. On reaching, retire to the allotted hotel and witness the evening aarti at bank of river ganga. Relax overnight at hotel.

Day 8: Varanasi – Kathmandu

In the early hours of morning, we take you on a exquisite boat ride on holy GangesAfter the sight-seeing of ghats and nearby places, we make sure that you board a flight to Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal. On reaching, you will be spending the night in the hotel there

Day 9: Kathmandu Sightseeing

In the early morning hours, you will be taken for the sight-seeing of Kathmandu. You will be visiting the Durbar Square thereafter and witness the Shah and Malla royalmansions. Reflecting the magnificent Nepalese architectural style makes your tour the more significant and worthwhile. Afternoon is kept for relaxation.

Day 10: Kathmandu Sightseeing

In the early hours of morning, we head towards Bhaktapur, one of the sacred places. Founded in 889 AD by King Ananda Deva, it is one of the magnificent cities. Situated in Kathmandu Valley, this mythical city is simply awesome from travel point of view. Thereafter we proceed to Patan to be followed by a trip to river Bagmati. We alsotake you to the Tibetan Refugee Craft Centre.

Day 11: Kathmandu - Delhi (Departure)

As we know the value of rest, this last day is specially kept to relax and take rest. You will be spending the last day of the tour relaxing to be followed with catching a flightback to Delhi. On reaching Delhi, you will take the next flight back home amidst due care and comfort.
